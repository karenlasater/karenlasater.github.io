# Karen Lasater ## Bio Lasater is a postdoctoral fellow at the School of Nursing [Center for Health Outcomes and Policy Research](http://www.nursing.upenn.edu/chopr/Pages/default.aspx) and a fellow at the [Leonard Davis Institute of Health Economics](http://ldi.upenn.edu/) at the University of Pennsylvania. Her dissertation examined the effects of hospital nursing on the likelihood of readmission for older adults following total hip and arthroplasty. Lasater's research interests include understanding: (1) how to organize nursing care to influence patient outcomes and promote safety and (2) how nurses add value to health care systems. ## Education * PhD - 2015, University of Pennsylvania * BSN - 2010, Quinnipiac University ## Publications * Brooks-Carthon, M., **Lasater, K.B.**, Rearden, J., Holland, S., & Sloane, D.M.
